Attachment 12 of the Apple Developer Program License Agreement has been revised to specify terms for iOS apps in Brazil, including alternative distribution, alternative payments and out-of-app offers, and the Core Technology Commission. Developers should review the changes and sign in to accept the updated terms; translations will be available on the Apple Developer website within one month.
