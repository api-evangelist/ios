Apple is opening its fifth Developer Center in Berlin, expanding beyond its existing locations in Cupertino, Shanghai, Bengaluru, and Singapore. The Berlin facility will provide European developers with in-person sessions, workshops, one-on-one appointments, consultation areas, and hands-on labs with Apple experts available in multiple languages. This new center aims to offer teams of all sizes better access to expert guidance and support throughout their app development journey.
