Due to a court ruling lifting an injunction on Texas law SB 2420, new Apple Accounts in Texas are now subject to age assurance requirements for app marketplaces starting June 4, 2026. These requirements include age verification and parental consent for minors under 18 for app downloads, in-app purchases, and significant app changes, with parents able to revoke consent at any time. Developers must implement the Declared Age Range API, Significant Change API (PermissionKit), and handle new App Store server notifications to comply with these requirements.
