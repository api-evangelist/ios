As part of an agreement with Brazil's competition regulator CADE, Apple is introducing changes to iOS that create new options for developers in Brazil beginning with iOS 26.5, including distributing apps on alternative app marketplaces, operating alternative marketplaces, and processing app payments for digital goods outside Apple In-App Purchase. Protections include Notarization for iOS apps, an authorization process for app marketplaces, and child-safety requirements; by July 6, 2026 all current Apple Developer Program members must agree to the latest License Agreement update.
